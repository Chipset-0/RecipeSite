Chicken Schnitzel

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast - 1kg
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Flour
  • Salt - 1Tbsp
  • Eggs x2
  • Cooking Oil
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Add Flour with Salt, Eggs and both Bread Crumbs to individual bowls
  2. Slice Chicken Breast into thin, flat slices
  3. Coat each chicken slice with the Flour Mixture, then the Egg, then the Bread crumbs. Leave the coated slice on a plate
  4. Add oil to frying pan. Add coated slices to the frying pan at high heat. If necessary add more oil.
  5. Add Mayonnaise to chciken Schnitzel, serve with salad
